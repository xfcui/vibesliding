## Shared Visual System

### Cover and Closing Slide Visual Treatment
- **Cover:** Dark background (Deep Space, `#0A0F1A`) with a centered, luminous heme-thiolate porphyrin ring rendered as a stylized geometric emblem — not photorealistic, but a glowing line-art motif in Electric Cyan (`#00E5FF`) with subtle Amber (`#FFB300`) accent nodes. Title text is large, vertically centered above the emblem. Subtitle, speaker name, affiliation, and contact details sit below in lighter weight. A faint grid of dots recedes into the background, suggesting a digital substrate.
- **Closing:** Mirrors the cover composition exactly but replaces the subtitle with a single takeaway sentence and adds contact details (WeChat, email) arranged as a minimal card in the lower third. The porphyrin emblem pulses slightly larger, signaling completion. A "Thank You / Q&A" label sits where the title was. The visual callback to the cover creates narrative closure.

### Transition / Roadmap Visual Pattern (MANDATORY)
- Every transition slide uses the **same section-map strip**: a horizontal pipeline of 6 labeled nodes (`01 / THE PROBLEM` through `06 / BEYOND SLIDES`) connected by thin lines, positioned in the lower third of the slide.
- The **current section's node** is rendered in full Electric Cyan (`#00E5FF`) glow with a filled circle; all other nodes are dim (`#3A4A5C` outline only).
- A **progress bar** runs beneath the node strip, filled proportionally to the current section (e.g., `progress bar 3/6` fills the bar 50%).
- The **upper two-thirds** of each transition slide displays the current section title in large bold text with a one-sentence section thesis beneath it in regular weight.
- Background remains Deep Space (`#0A0F1A`) with a faint radial gradient brightening slightly behind the active node, creating a spotlight effect.
- Recurring three-pinned-dots motif (from the porphyrin emblem) appears as a subtle watermark at 15% opacity in the upper-right corner of every transition slide.

### Diagram Language
- **Boxes:** Rounded-corner rectangles with 1px Electric Cyan (`#00E5FF`) borders on dark backgrounds, or 1px Slate (`#3A4A5C`) borders for de-emphasized elements. Fill is semi-transparent (`#0A0F1A` at 80% opacity) to let the background grid show through.
- **Arrows and connectors:** Thin (1.5px) lines in Electric Cyan for primary flow, dashed Amber (`#FFB300`) for alternative/fallback paths. Arrowheads are small, triangular, filled.
- **Icons:** Minimal line-art style (not filled/solid), consistent 24px stroke weight, using the Electric Cyan / Amber / Signal Green palette. Tool-specific icons (Cursor, OpenRouter, Valyu) use simplified logomarks, not full logos.
- **Glow rules:** Key focal elements (the active pipeline stage, the selected contact-sheet variant, the current section node) receive a soft 8px outer glow in their accent color at 40% opacity. No glow on secondary or background elements.
- **Data visualizations:** Bar charts use filled Electric Cyan bars with Amber comparison bars. Threshold/target lines are dashed Signal Green (`#00E676`). Axis labels in Slate text. No 3D effects, no gradients within bars.

### Recurring Motifs and Section Color Accents
- **Heme-thiolate porphyrin ring:** A simplified geometric version of this molecular structure appears at 15% opacity as a background watermark on every content and transition slide, positioned in the upper-right corner. On the cover and closing slides it is the primary visual.
- **Three-pinned-dots:** Three small dots arranged in a triangle (abstracted from the porphyrin's iron-coordination geometry) serve as a section-break ornament and appear on transition slides as part of the watermark.
- **Pipeline thread line:** A thin horizontal Electric Cyan line runs across the very bottom of every content slide (2px, 30% opacity), visually threading the deck together. On transition slides this line expands into the full progress bar.
- **Section color accents** (used for section-title text on transition slides and for highlight badges on content slides within that section):
  - Section 1 — THE PROBLEM: Signal Red (`#FF5252`)
  - Section 2 — THE ARCHITECTURE: Electric Cyan (`#00E5FF`)
  - Section 3 — THE PIPELINE: Amber (`#FFB300`)
  - Section 4 — VISUAL CURATION: Vivid Purple (`#B388FF`)
  - Section 5 — QUALITY & COST: Signal Green (`#00E676`)
  - Section 6 — BEYOND SLIDES: Warm White (`#FFFDE7`)
- **Slide number format:** Every slide displays `N/total` in the bottom-right corner in Slate (`#3A4A5C`) text, using the monospace font at 10pt. Cover and closing slides omit the number.

## Appendix: Global Visual Requirements

- **Theme:**
  - Background: Deep Space `#0A0F1A`
  - Primary Accent: Electric Cyan `#00E5FF`
  - Secondary Accent: Amber `#FFB300`
  - Tertiary Accent: Vivid Purple `#B388FF`
  - Positive / Success: Signal Green `#00E676`
  - Negative / Warning: Signal Red `#FF5252`
  - De-emphasis / Borders: Slate `#3A4A5C`
  - Body Text: Cool White `#E0E6ED`
  - Highlight Text: Warm White `#FFFDE7`
- **Fonts:**
  - Titles: Inter Bold, 36pt (cover), 28pt (content/transition slide titles)
  - Subtitles: Inter SemiBold, 20pt
  - Body bullets: Inter Regular, 16pt
  - Labels and annotations: Inter Medium, 14pt
  - Code / CLI / monospace: JetBrains Mono Regular, 14pt
  - Speaker name / affiliation: Inter Light, 18pt
- **Format:** 16:9 aspect ratio (1920 × 1080 px), 5% safe margin on all edges, minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for all text against background
